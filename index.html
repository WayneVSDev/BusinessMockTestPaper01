Section C: Case Study (30 marks)

Case Study: FreshFarm Co. FreshFarm Co. is a small business started by Anna, who grows organic vegetables on her family’s land. She sells her produce at local markets and has recently hired two employees to help with harvesting and sales. Anna operates as a Sole Trader but is considering forming a Partnership with her brother to expand the business. Her goals are to increase revenue and ensure the business remains environmentally sustainable. However, she’s worried about competition from larger farms and changing weather patterns due to climate change.
